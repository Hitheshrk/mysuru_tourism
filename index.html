Welcome to Mysuru

Mysuru (Mysore) is a historic city in Karnataka, India, known for its rich cultural heritage, royal legacy, and architectural marvels. It was the capital of the Wadiyar dynasty and is famous for the grand Mysore Palace, a stunning Indo-Saracenic structure illuminated with thousands of lights during the Dasara festival.Tourists visit Mysuru for attractions like the Chamundi Hill Temple, Brindavan Gardens, St. Philomenaâ€™s Church, and the Mysuru Zoo. The city is also known for its silk sarees, sandalwood products, and the famous Mysore Pak sweet.

How to Reach Mysuru

Mysuru is well connected by road, rail, and air. It is around 150 km from Bengaluru and has regular bus and train services.
